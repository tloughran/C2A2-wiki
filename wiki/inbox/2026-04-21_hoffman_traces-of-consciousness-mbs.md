## Summary
Hoffman's fourth appearance on Mind-Body Solution — nearly three hours of the most significant update yet to Conscious Realism. He introduces trace logic, decorated permutations, and the emergence of spacetime from networks of conscious agents, describing what he calls the strongest mathematical progress so far toward deriving Minkowski space from consciousness itself. The episode includes a dedicated segment, "Why Spacetime Is Doomed: The 2026 Case," setting out Hoffman's current argument that spacetime is not fundamental and must be replaced by a deeper consciousness-first ontology.

## Why This Matters for This Tradition
This is the most significant public elaboration of Hoffman's post-spacetime program since the 2026 Prentner collaboration already in the wiki. The trace-order formalism and decorated permutations are new technical machinery not yet captured in existing PRS triplets. It also moves Hoffman from arguing *that* spacetime is doomed toward specifying *how* Minkowski structure would be recovered from networks of conscious agents — a concrete mathematical commitment the tradition can be held to.

## Cross-Tradition Signals
- **Arkani-Hamed / positive geometry:** decorated permutations are the same combinatorial objects that appear in the positive Grassmannian / amplituhedron program. Hoffman is explicitly leaning on positive-geometry-style machinery to build a consciousness-first physics. This is a strong CROSS-candidate.
- **Wolfram / Ruliad:** both Hoffman and Wolfram now posit a fundamental non-spacetime substrate (trace order vs. ruliad) from which spacetime is recovered as an observer interface.
- **Kastrup / analytic idealism:** converging on consciousness as ontologically prior, but via different formalisms — worth flagging as a potential explanatory-bridge candidate.
