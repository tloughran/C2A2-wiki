## Summary
Michael Levin appeared on Mayim Bialik's Breakdown podcast on March 10, 2026, in a 1 hour 50 minute interview covering his current bioelectricity and diverse intelligence research. Key framings: Levin describes cancer as "dissociative identity disorder of the body" — a breakdown in bioelectrical network coherence rather than a genetic event — and argues that "mind blindness" (our inability to recognize intelligence in non-human substrates) is the primary epistemic obstacle to next-generation biomedicine and AI.
