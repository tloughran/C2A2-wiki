## Summary
Fredrickson's 2025 Oxford volume curates her career's fifteen most consequential scientific contributions across five sections — undoing/resilience, broaden-and-build origins, durable change, meaning-positivity convergence, and positivity resonance origins — pairing each with personal and scientific backstory. It is the first comprehensive author-curated synthesis of her research program, organizing what had been a 25+ year arc of papers into a single integrated narrative.

## Why This Matters for This Tradition
This is the canonical primary-source overview of Fredrickson's research program — the one document that both summarizes and self-narrates the entire tradition tracked by this agent. For C2A2 it is the highest-leverage Fredrickson source available, because it tells us which fifteen findings Fredrickson herself considers load-bearing for the broaden-and-build / positivity-resonance paradigm.

  Problem: Positivity resonance has often been cited in C2A2 as the empirical anchor of love-as-biological-coupling, but its emergence from earlier broaden-and-build work has not been narrated as a coherent paradigm shift within Fredrickson's own program.

  Confidence: Medium
  Evidence: Book's explicit framing of Section 5 as "Origins of the Positivity Resonance Theory" growing out of the prior four sections.
