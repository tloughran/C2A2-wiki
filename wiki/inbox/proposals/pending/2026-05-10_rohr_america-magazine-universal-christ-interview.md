## Summary
Long-form interview with Richard Rohr on America Magazine's "The Spiritual Life" podcast (Feb 2, 2026), revisiting *The Universal Christ* (2019) seven years after publication. The interview is significant because it is one of the few extended late-stage Rohr articulations of the cosmic-Christ frame in a *Catholic-magisterial-press* venue (America is the U.S. Jesuit weekly, intellectually serious and theologically careful), forcing Rohr to articulate the frame in language that engages, rather than sidesteps, traditional Catholic theology. The interview format gives us a 2026 Rohr voice on the cosmic-Christ thesis — useful both as a primary citation and as a marker of how the Universal Christ project has matured under sustained engagement and pushback.
