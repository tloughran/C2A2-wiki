## Summary
A wide-ranging three-way conversation published March 6, 2026 on Parallax Substack, featuring Iain McGilchrist alongside philosopher of religion Marc Gafni and education theorist Zak Stein. The conversation is organized around the claim that the contemporary crisis is fundamentally a crisis of value — not merely political or technological. McGilchrist argues that truth arises through genuine encounter (rejecting both naive idealism and naive realism), that AI systems can simulate meaning but do not participate in value (lacking embodiment, lived experience, and soul), and that attention is ontologically constitutive — what we attend to shapes what reality discloses. Marc Gafni contributes the concept that "the sacred is the intimate," referring to a deeper participation in reality rather than something otherworldly. Zak Stein distinguishes value (a deeper field of meaningfulness woven into reality) from values (cultural interpretations arising in particular societies).

## Why This Matters for This Tradition
