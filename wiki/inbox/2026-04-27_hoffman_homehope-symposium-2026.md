## Summary
Donald Hoffman appeared as a featured speaker at the HOMeHOPe Virtual Symposium 2026 (April 23–24), with content emphasizing his interface theory of perception, conscious-agent ontology, and recent extensions involving "traces of consciousness" — the same line of work Hoffman opened in the Mind Body Solution dialogue ingested earlier this month (PROP-2026-04-21). The symposium framing reportedly extends Hoffman's interface theory to questions of space-time as compressed user interface, evolution of perception under fitness-beats-truth pressure, and the reality status of psychedelic experience as glimpses outside the interface.

## Why This Matters for This Tradition
This is the second major Hoffman public appearance within April 2026 and provides a fresh, recent restatement of the conscious-agent ontology. It is significant because Hoffman has been consolidating "traces of consciousness" as the formal connective tissue between his interface theory and the Markov-blanket / dissociative-boundary network already mapped in the wiki (FINDING-009, FINDING-011 SUPER-BRIDGE). A fresh symposium-format presentation provides a higher-density compressed version of the framework than long-form podcasts and is a useful fixed reference point against which to test whether the trace-logic story has stabilized or is still evolving in Hoffman's hands.

## Candidate PRS Triplets

PRS-CANDIDATE-01:
  Problem: Despite the formal apparatus of conscious agents and traces of consciousness, Hoffman's framework still owes the field a clean, presentable answer to: how does the projection from consciousness-only ontology produce something that *looks like* an objective spacetime to embedded observers?
  Resource: HOMeHOPe symposium framing of space-time as user-interface-compressed — pairing the interface theory of perception with explicit appeals to data-compression and information-bottleneck arguments. This is the most condensed public formulation to date of "spacetime is doomed" → "interface compression" → "traces of consciousness" as a single argument chain.
  Solution: A symposium-grade single-pass narrative that the wiki can use as a citable canonical statement of the Hoffman position circa April 2026, replacing the longer scattered formulations across the November 2025 Bialik podcast, the Prentner quantum-interface paper, and the Mind Body Solution dialogue.
  Confidence: Medium
  Evidence: Symposium listing and prior HOMeHOPe Hoffman content on the same site indicating canonical framing.

PRS-CANDIDATE-02:
  Problem: The conscious-agent ontology has been criticized as un-falsifiable and as silently assuming the very physical regularities it claims to derive. Where does Hoffman now locate empirical purchase for the framework?
  Resource: Hoffman's symposium-context appeals to evolutionary fitness simulations (FBT theorem family) as empirical support, and to anomalies in psychedelic-state phenomenology as candidate data outside the interface. Combined, these are presented as a two-pronged empirical research program rather than a purely metaphysical thesis.
  Solution: An updated empirical-program statement that pairs (a) computational-evolutionary modeling of perception under fitness pressure, with (b) phenomenological data from altered states as candidate "interface-leak" observations. This is the cleanest version yet of how Hoffman wants the framework treated as science rather than metaphysics.
  Confidence: Speculative
  Evidence: Symposium framing; HOMeHOPe podcast page listing "Consciousness Theories, Space-Time, Evolution, and Psychedelics" as the Hoffman content pillar.
