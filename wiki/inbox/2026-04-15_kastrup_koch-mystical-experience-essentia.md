## Summary
Published February 20, 2026 on Essentia Foundation, this conversation features neuroscientist Christof Koch, philosopher Bernardo Kastrup, and moderator Hans Busstra exploring what it means to take mystical experience seriously without abandoning scientific rigor. Koch — one of the world's leading consciousness researchers and a proponent of Integrated Information Theory (IIT) — engages directly with Kastrup's analytic idealism. The conversation addresses the convergence between psychedelic experiences across individuals (potential archetypal universals), the dangers and promises of idealism as a metaphysical framework, and whether rigorous science can accommodate the ontological primacy of consciousness.
