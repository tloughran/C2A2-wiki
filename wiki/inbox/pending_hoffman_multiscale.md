## Quick Summary (3 sentences)

Hoffman and Prakash present trace logic — a non-Boolean partial order on Markov chains that serves as the logic of minimal surprise — now extended recursively to yield a novel, formally precise notion of agency. They connect this framework to causal emergence, Friston's Markov blankets (as a limiting special case), quantum logic, and the positive geometry program in physics, arguing that spacetime, GR, and QM are all emergent descriptions of conscious agent dynamics. The talk is hosted by Michael Levin on Thoughtforms Life, with participation from Chris Fields and Robert Chis-Ciure.
