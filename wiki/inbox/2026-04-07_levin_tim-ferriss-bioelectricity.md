## Summary
Michael Levin appeared on The Tim Ferriss Show (Episode #849, January 21, 2026) for a long-form interview covering the full scope of his bioelectricity research program. Levin describes how cells communicate and store information through bioelectric signals independent of DNA, leading to discoveries about how organisms develop, regenerate, and maintain their form. He presents a vision where biological systems can be reprogrammed like software — updating cellular instructions to heal, regenerate, and potentially reverse aging and disease without genetic modification.

## Candidate PRS Triplets

PRS-CANDIDATE-01:
  Problem: Current medicine treats disease by targeting DNA/hardware (genes, proteins) while ignoring the bioelectric "software" layer that coordinates cell behavior
  Resource: Bioelectric reprogramming — manipulating voltage patterns in cell networks to change their collective behavior without altering DNA
  Solution: A new therapeutic paradigm: treat cancer, birth defects, and aging by updating bioelectric instructions rather than genetic code — demonstrated in planaria, frogs, and early mammalian models
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: Levin describes multiple experimental systems where bioelectric manipulation achieves outcomes impossible through genetic approaches alone

PRS-CANDIDATE-02:
  Problem: The mechanism by which non-neural cells exhibit goal-directed behavior (cognition of cells) remains poorly understood by mainstream biology
  Resource: The concept of cells as cognitive agents navigating "anatomical morphospace" — using bioelectric signals as a proto-cognitive medium for storing and processing information about body form
  Solution: A framework that treats cell collectives as problem-solving agents, opening regenerative medicine to top-down (information-level) interventions rather than bottom-up (molecular) manipulation
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: Levin's description of xenobots and planarian regeneration experiments as demonstrations of cellular collective intelligence
