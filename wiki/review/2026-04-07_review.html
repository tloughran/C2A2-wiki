https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.00001
SUMMARY

The paper formalizes the claim that any system maintaining a Markov blanket — a statistical boundary separating internal from external states — necessarily minimizes free energy. This makes FEP formally substrate-independent: it applies to neurons, cells, organisms, and potentially AI systems.

SUMMARY

Crucially, he foregrounds the observer's role: observers embedded within the Ruliad necessarily perceive it in particular ways, and our "inner experiences" must be represented within it. This essay was written partly to launch the Wolfram Institute's Templeton-funded "Computational Metaphysics" project.
