## Triplets

PRS-01:
  Label: P1 — Dualistic knowing
  Problem: Western rationalism defaults to either/or categorization, fragmenting reality into opposing camps and making genuine integration impossible
  Resource: The contemplative tradition's practice of non-dual awareness — holding both/and without premature resolution
  Solution: Contemplative epistemology as a complement to analytical reason; mature consciousness perceives unity-in-distinction rather than collapsing into false dichotomies
  Date Added: 2026-04-29
  Source: Everything Belongs (1999); The Naked Now (2009)
  Confidence: High

PRS-02:
  Label: P2 — The disorder necessary for growth
  Problem: Intellectual and spiritual traditions resist disruption, treating challenges to received order as threats rather than opportunities for deeper understanding
  Resource: The order-disorder-reorder pattern observed across contemplative traditions, developmental psychology, and the history of science
  Solution: Epistemological crises are not failures but necessary passages; the reorder that follows genuine disorder is deeper than the original order because it has been tested and survived
  Date Added: 2026-04-29
  Source: Falling Upward (2011); parallels MacIntyre's epistemological crisis
  Confidence: High

PRS-03:
  Label: P3 — The Universal Christ
  Problem: If truth is confined to one tradition, inter-tradition dialogue is at best diplomatic compromise and at worst intellectual imperialism
  Resource: The Franciscan theological claim that the divine pattern (the Christ) is present in all of creation, not confined to any single tradition or institutional expression
  Solution: A theological warrant for genuine multi-tradition inquiry — encountering truth through biology, mathematics, philosophy, and contemplation simultaneously is not eclecticism but a recognition of the universality of the pattern
  Date Added: 2026-04-29
  Source: The Universal Christ (2019)
  Confidence: Medium

PRS-04:
  Label: P4 — Embodied love as epistemology
  Problem: Abstract theological claims about love remain disconnected from empirical observation and biological reality
  Resource: Franciscan incarnational theology — the insistence that the spiritual is always embodied, never purely abstract
  Solution: Love as a biophysiological event (connecting to Fredrickson's positivity resonance) and a contemplative practice, not merely a theological concept; the body is a site of knowing
  Date Added: 2026-04-29
  Source: The Universal Christ, Chapter 6; connection to Fredrickson Love 2.0
  Confidence: Medium
