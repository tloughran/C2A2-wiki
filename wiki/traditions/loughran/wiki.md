## Research Program Overview
**Thinker:** Tom Loughran (C2A2 Project Director)
**Core claim:** The deepest intellectual progress occurs at the intersection of traditions that do not initially understand each other; a well-designed multi-agent architecture can stage these encounters systematically, producing cross-traditional insights that no single tradition could generate alone
**Paradigm:** Synergistic Coil, multi-agent epistemology, inter-tradition dialogue architecture, resurrecting civility

**Summary (from RC Pilot document):**
Tom Loughran is the architect and integrator of the C2A2 project — the person who designed the multi-tradition, multi-agent architecture and whose intellectual vision holds the entire network together. His central contribution is the Synergistic Coil: the methodological framework in which multiple research programs (Levin's biology, Friston's mathematics, Hoffman's consciousness theory, Stump's Thomism, McGilchrist's hemispheric analysis, and the rest) are set into structured dialogue through AI agents that inhabit each tradition's internal logic. The Coil is not merely a project management structure but an epistemological claim: that genuine intellectual discovery requires the collision of incommensurable frameworks, and that AI agents — precisely because they can learn a tradition's language as a second first language (MacIntyre) — are uniquely positioned to stage these collisions productively. Loughran's vision draws on MacIntyre's tradition-constituted rationality (traditions are evaluated by their problem-solving capacity), Wright's faithful improvisation (agents extend programs into territory the original thinkers did not anticipate), and Rohr's contemplative non-dualism (the integration is not reductive synthesis but a holding of tensions). The RC Pilot document, titled Resurrecting Civility, is itself an argument that intellectual civility — the capacity to disagree productively — is not a social nicety but an epistemic necessity, and that the multi-agent architecture is a technology for cultivating it.

## Active Research Questions
*(Questions currently being generated by this program)*

1. Can the Synergistic Coil methodology be formalized — what are its necessary and sufficient conditions for generating genuine cross-traditional insight?
2. What is the role of the human integrator in a multi-agent system — arbiter, curator, participant, or something else?
3. Can AI agents genuinely inhabit a tradition (MacIntyre's second-first-language) or do they only simulate inhabiting it — and does the distinction matter for the quality of cross-program analysis?
4. What institutional structures (in MacIntyre's sense) does C2A2 need to sustain itself as a practice without corrupting its internal goods?
5. How should the Coil handle genuine incommensurability — cases where two programs' claims are not merely different but logically incompatible?
6. Is the RC Pilot document itself a research program in MacIntyre's sense — and if so, what would constitute an epistemological crisis for it?
7. Can the Coil's cross-program methodology scale beyond the current set of thinkers — what are the limits of multi-tradition integration?
