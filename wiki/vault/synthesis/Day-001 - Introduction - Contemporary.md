# Day 1 — Contemporary Parallel: *What a Summa Is, Now That We Know What a Wiki Is*

When Aquinas opens the *Summa* by saying it is for *beginners*, he is doing something specific: naming a form of inquiry that holds together because it serves a single learning trajectory rather than a catalogue of expert results. The contemporary analog appeared, of all places, in an April 2026 GitHub gist. Andrej Karpathy proposed an "LLM Wiki" — a persistent, agent-maintained knowledge base in which findings *compound* over time rather than evaporating between sessions. The point was not the chatbot; the point was that knowledge has to be *kept*, not merely produced. Within weeks the shape spread, and the C2A2 Wiki — the one feeding this synthesis — is one instance: eleven named research-program "traditions" (Levin, Friston, Hoffman, Hawkins, McGilchrist, Fredrickson, Stump, Carroll, Arkani-Hamed, Wolfram, Kastrup), plus two ground-truth oracles being added now (Wright on Scripture, Rohr on contemplation), each with its own sub-wiki, knit together by a Master Agent and a Pattern Detector that watch for cross-tradition resonances. A *Summa* in our idiom is not a 4,000-page printed book; it is a wiki graph any beginner can drop into, follow one trajectory, and gather the gist. Same architecture, different substrate.

## Purpose

Aquinas's stated purpose was the instruction of beginners — explicitly, not the display of erudition. The contemporary version is encoded in the project's own scaffolding: the **PRS triplet** — Problem, Resource, Solution — the unit Tom Loughran uses to describe and interlink each tradition's work, with the **Synergistic Coil** as its meta-tool, a Resource that resolves more than one Problem→Solution transition at once. The framework sits at the convergence of three lineages. **Kuhn**: the solved problem is the unit of scientific progress. **MacIntyre**: traditions are defined by narratives of shared goals, problems arising in pursuit of them, resources drawn on, and solutions achieved, promised, or yet to be realized. **Levin** (with William James in the deeper background): intelligence is problem-solving with limited resources used to solve multiple problems — which is why a single Resource bridging multiple P⇒S transitions does not violate scarcity, it *exploits* it. The eleven `prs_triplets.md` files in C2A2 are not each tradition's self-description; they are Tom's (and the network's) PRS-form re-description of those traditions, each thinker's work re-framed in shared language so it can be seen alongside the others. Aquinas defends himself in the Prologue against "the multiplication of useless questions"; the contemporary architect of a tradition-accelerator scaffold defends against the charge of dilettantism. The defenses run parallel — not brevity for its own sake but *tractable entry into the real*, enough that the beginner can soon hold the whole shape in mind.

## The modern magisterial scaffold

Aquinas leans on three modern magisterial documents — *Aeterni Patris* (1879), *Pascendi Dominici Gregis* (1907), *Fides et Ratio* (1998) — to establish that this kind of inquiry is *worth* doing. The contemporary scaffold has its own three. **Kuhn's** claim that the solved problem is the unit of scientific progress: what makes a body of work one science is not its doctrinal core but the chain of puzzles it has actually solved. **MacIntyre's** tradition-vitality criterion: a tradition is live if it can solve its own problems, identify genuine anomalies, and engage external challenges from internal coherence — a tradition that abandons its founding questions ceases to be that tradition. **Karpathy's** compounding-knowledge thesis: knowledge has to be persistently structured or it dissipates between sessions. Each plays the role *Aeterni Patris*, *Pascendi*, and *Fides et Ratio* play for Aquinas — a test by which a body of inquiry is judged, not by its conclusions, but by its *shape*. Note the close echo of Pius X warning that one cannot "set St. Thomas aside, especially in metaphysical questions, without grave detriment." Kuhn says the same thing in epistemological idiom: you cannot quietly drop the chain of solved problems and still claim to be doing the same science. John Paul II's *Fides et Ratio* warned that faith without reason withers into superstition and reason without faith loses contact with the radicality of being; the contemporary parallel is the warning that follows when a research program detaches from its empirical wing or its metaphysical wing. The two-wings image survives the translation almost intact — just with different birds carrying the load.

## Authors

Aquinas drew attention to three things about himself: saint, Common Doctor, Dominican. Each maps. *Saint*: the inquiry was not done for no purpose but because the author *loved* its object. The contemporary equivalent is the long-form research program — Levin three decades into bioelectricity, Friston three decades into the free energy principle, Hoffman three decades into the interface theory of perception, Stump four decades into rehabilitating Thomistic hylomorphism. Pius XI's biographical detail — Aquinas leaning his head against the tabernacle, fasting, spending nights in prayer — is structurally what Friston means when he says active inference is a *form of life*, not a publication strategy. *Common Doctor*: tradition-spanning synthesis. The C2A2 Master Agent and its Pattern Detector are the explicit attempt at this, tracking which questions appear in more than one program, flagging where one tradition has already solved a problem the other is still chasing. CROSS-001 (Is consciousness substrate-independent?) is the highest-density question across five programs; CROSS-002 (Is spacetime fundamental?) tracks three. *Dominican*: evangelistic, practical, not academic-for-its-own-sake. This is where Wright and Rohr enter — Wright as the contemporary scriptural ground-truth oracle (faith resting on a body of historically-investigable claims about Israel and Jesus, not just propositions), Rohr as the contemporary contemplative ground-truth oracle (the *Universal Christ* frame — divine pattern present in matter from creation, not just in a 2,000-year-old human). These two are not metaphysicians but pastors of method. Where the medieval Dominican preached on the road, the contemporary tradition-accelerator wiki publishes its findings into ordinary discourse so that the beginner, the parish-level reader, the practitioner — not just the expert — gets to compound the inquiry.

## The metaphysical reframe

The *Summa* asks, eventually, the question of what is fundamental. The contemporary frame does not answer in Aquinas's terms; it *reframes*. Levin shows that form is empirically real and scales — bioelectric pattern as a literal causal substrate, not a metaphor. Hoffman shows that perceived spacetime is a species-specific user interface, not the underlying reality. Kastrup argues that mind is the ontological primitive and matter is what experience looks like from the outside. Together they describe a mind-everywhere monist ontology that *reframes* hylomorphism rather than mapping onto it cleanly. PRS-04 in the Stump-tradition wiki — Tom's record of the bold move from the Thomistic side, that final causality formally maps onto Levin's morphogenetic teleology — is the bridge the C2A2 network has flagged as FLAG-005 (highest priority), the most concrete current crossing between a 13th-century metaphysical claim and a 21st-century empirical finding. But the metaphysical guardrail in this vault is explicit: where the *Summa* asks "what is the one truth," the lead voices are Levin, Hoffman, and Kastrup; Stump joins as a Thomistic interlocutor *receiving* the reframe rather than authorizing it. The Pius X warning — that one cannot set Aquinas aside in metaphysical questions without grave detriment — survives, but with the weight redistributed: the medieval framework is preserved as a question-generator and a discipline of attention, not as the authoritative answer.

## The hunter image

Chesterton's Aquinas dreamed like a hunting dog: not pursuing systems for their own sake but tracking error to its lair. The contemporary version is the Pattern Detector Agent — the C2A2 component whose only job is to flag where two programs are using different vocabularies for the same underlying problem. FLAG-003 is the live exemplar: Kastrup's dissociative boundary may be formally identical to Friston's Markov blanket, which would make analytic idealism and the free-energy principle two vocabularies for one set of structural dynamics — the first consciousness-physics formal equivalence in the network. The Manichee Aquinas pounded the table about was a real intellectual error. The contemporary Manichees are quieter — incommensurable vocabularies, claims of incommunicability, premature truces between traditions. The hunter does not build cathedrals of system; he names where the quarry is. Every confirmed FLAG in the C2A2 network is, in effect, a **Synergistic Coil** candidate: a single R bridging two or more tradition-internal P⇒S chains at once. The product is not the *Summa*; it is the aliveness with which beginners are still finding their way *in*.

Convergence of Kuhn (problem as unit of progress), MacIntyre (tradition as P/R/S narrative), Levin via James (problem-solving under resource scarcity). The form in which Tom describes and interlinks every tradition.

- **FLAG-005 (Stump × Levin)** — final causality formally mapping onto Levin's morphogenetic teleology — the cleanest current Synergistic Coil candidate (one R bridging Stump's metaphysical and Levin's empirical P⇒S chains).

## Where this leaves us

The Prologue asks no specific question. Tomorrow's episode does — *Does God exist?* — and the metaphysical guardrail kicks in for the first time. The synthesis there will not lead with Stump but with Carroll (poetic naturalism as the foil), Kastrup (idealism as the contemporary positive answer), Hoffman (interface theory's reframe of the question itself), and Arkani-Hamed (what fundamental physics treats as primitive). Stump returns as Thomistic interlocutor, not metaphysical authority. Day 1 is a frame; from Day 2, the weight begins to bear.
