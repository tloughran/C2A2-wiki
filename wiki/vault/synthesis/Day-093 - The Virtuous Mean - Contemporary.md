# Day 93 — The Virtuous Mean (Contemporary)

## Frame

Aquinas asks two paired questions: *what is the mean that virtue observes?* and *how are the virtues connected to one another?* The contemporary parallel under mind/conscious-realist monism asks: **what is the optimization criterion the limited agent is solving when it acts virtuously, and what is the topology of the disposition-network that solves it?** Aquinas's answer to the first is *conformity to right reason* (with a careful bifurcation between *real* and *rational* mean). His answer to the second is *charity* (and *prudence* among the natural virtues) integrates everything. The contemporary frame holds both answers but re-describes the mean as an active-inference objective and the integration as a network-topology fact.

## Problem

The agent confronts a continuous, multidimensional space of possible actions and affective states. *Virtue* picks out, for each domain, a *fit-with-reason* region — not always a literal midpoint, sometimes total abstinence — that the agent should occupy. But the agent's natural appetites and affects are calibrated to particular ranges, individually variable, and entangled across domains. Two problems compound: **(P1)** how is the mean *individuated* across kinds of action? **(P2)** what binds the individuated virtues into a coherent agent — so that prudence doesn't drift away from justice, fortitude away from temperance? Aquinas's answer to P1 is the real/rational-mean bifurcation; his answer to P2 is the connection-of-virtues doctrine.

## Resource

Friston's active inference framework supplies a direct formalization of P1. The "mean" of a moral action is an **optimal policy under constraints**: the action that minimizes expected free energy given the agent's generative model. Two distinct sub-cases drop out:

- **Exogenous-precision case (Aquinas's real mean, e.g., justice in commercial exchange):** the constraint is supplied by an *objective social fact* — the price, the contract, the debt. Precision-weighting on the external evidence is high; the agent's internal affective state contributes little to the optimum.
- **Endogenous-precision case (Aquinas's rational mean, e.g., temperance, fortitude):** the constraint is supplied by the *individual's own affective generative model*. The same external food, the same external danger, calls for different policies in different agents. Precision-weighting on internal-state evidence is high.

Aquinas's bifurcation (Q.64.2) is exactly Friston's distinction. Empirically robust at the modeling level.

PRS-04 in the Stump-tradition wiki: virtue-as-habituation is a *stable parameterization* of the agent's generative model, not a mere disposition. The Thomistic claim that virtue takes time to form and degrades under disuse fits the active-inference picture: stable parameterizations require repeated evidence and decay under repeated counter-evidence.

PRS-02 in the Fredrickson-tradition wiki: positivity resonance is an empirically supported mechanism for *coupling* across agents and across an agent's own affective subsystems. The broaden-and-build account predicts that high-quality positive interactions widen the action repertoire (broaden) and build durable inter- and intrapersonal resources. Mixed-but-meaningful empirical support: the broadening effect is replicated; long-term build effects are real but smaller than initial estimates.

Levin (multi-scale agency, Levin 2022) and FLAG-09 (cross-program index): competence at one scale presupposes competence at adjacent scales. The connection-of-virtues claim is a special case of a general fact about scaled cognition: hub-capacities co-emerge with their satellites. Empirically firm.

Wright (canonical: *Paul and the Faithfulness of God*) and Rohr (canonical: *Universal Christ*): charity is the **integration hub** of the limited agent's whole disposition network, not just one virtue among the others. Wright's reading of agape as covenant-loyalty made cruciformly concrete, and Rohr's reading of charity as non-dual loving attention, agree on the structural claim: cut charity, the rest of the disposition-network fragments.

## Solution (proposed, speculative)

The mean of virtue is the **optimal action-and-affect policy** for the limited agent under its constraints — internal (its generative model, its affective parameterization) and external (the relational and physical world it acts in). The bifurcation Aquinas marks at Q.64.2 is real and corresponds to whether the optimization is dominated by *exogenous* or *endogenous* precision-weighting.

The connection of the virtues (Q.65) is the *network-topology* fact that hub-dispositions co-vary with their satellites. There are two distinct claims here:

- **Among natural moral virtues**: prudence is the network hub. Cut prudence, the others lose their orientation. Friston's hierarchical active inference predicts exactly this: the policy-selection layer must be functional for lower-level habit selection to remain coherent.
- **Among infused virtues**: charity is the network hub. Cut charity, the infused virtues fragment. Wright's covenant-loyalty reading and Rohr's non-dual contemplative reading converge: charity is the unifying *direction-of-relation* without which the rest of the network drifts.

**What this shares with Aquinas**: the formal-object individuation; the real/rational mean bifurcation; the hub-and-spokes topology of the virtue network.

**Where this reframes**: the *ontology* of "right reason." Aquinas treats right reason as a *normative* fact about the rational order. The contemporary frame reads right reason as the *optimal-inference-and-action policy* for the limited agent — normative, but normative in the way that "the policy that best minimizes prediction error given the agent's commitments and situation" is normative. This is not anti-realist; under mind/conscious-realist monism, the policy is normative because the underlying reality (mind, consciousness) is normatively structured.

**Where evidence is firm vs. speculative**: Friston's precision-weighting framework — empirically robust at the modeling level. The hub-and-spokes virtue-network topology — formally consistent with coupled-trait psychometrics; empirical recovery of the specific Thomistic hub (prudence) is suggestive, not decisive. Charity-as-hub of the infused-virtue network — Wright/Rohr theological-historical territory; not natural-science-empirical. The integration is speculative.

## The central-theme thread

The limited agent's computation toward loving unity is constrained at every step by what it can perceive and feel. The mean of virtue names the *good policy under those constraints* — not a universal midpoint, not a stoic perfection-point. The connection of the virtues names the *network-fact* that good policies in one domain depend on good policies in adjacent domains. Cut justice and prudence drifts; cut charity and the infused virtues fragment. This is why we *work out our salvation in fear and trembling*: the agent's whole disposition-network must remain coupled, and coupling is fragile. **We need each other** — and we need the contemplative tradition's testimony to charity's hub-role — precisely because no agent can hold the network coherent alone.

## Bridges

- **Friston (active inference, precision-weighting):** formal account of Q.64.2's real/rational-mean bifurcation. Formal/empirical.
- **Stump, PRS-04 (virtue-as-habituation):** the Thomistic claim about stable parameterization of the agent's generative model. Theological-philosophical.
- **Fredrickson, PRS-02 (positivity resonance, broaden-and-build):** empirical mechanism for cross-agent and intra-agent coupling. Empirical (mixed-strong).
- **Levin, PRS-03 (multi-scale agency):** general framework in which hub-and-spokes virtue-network is a special case. Empirical.
- **Wright (canonical) + Rohr (canonical):** charity as integration-hub of the infused-virtue network. Theological.
- **CROSS-09 (master index, network-of-virtues):** Tom's Synergistic Coil — coupled-network formalism solves Aquinas's connection-of-virtues, Friston's hierarchical inference, and Levin's multi-scale agency simultaneously.

## Where this leaves us

Q.66 tomorrow asks about the **equality among the virtues** and which is greatest. The connection-of-virtues claim from today implies that "greatest" can only mean *hub-most* — the virtue that, if cut, collapses the most of the rest. Tomorrow's answer will be charity (for the infused network) and a more nuanced answer (intellectual > moral; wisdom > knowledge; justice as chief moral virtue) for the natural network. The Q.67 question — *which virtues survive death?* — will be the eschatological filter that tests our contemporary reframe most sharply.
